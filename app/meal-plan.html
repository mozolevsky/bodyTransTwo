Who’s this meal plan for?

This meal plan is best for anyone who lives an active lifestyle, is trying to lose weight, or trying to adapt ahealthier lifestyle. It’s designed to cater to busy people who don’t have much time on their hands to plan and cook meals every day.

When people are short on time, stressed and hungry, often they will reach for the most convenient food option, but this doesn’t fuel their bodies properly. Whether you are not eating enough food throughout the day or eating the wrong foods, it can result in a decreased energy level, weight gain, and can increase your risk of injury and disease.

Nourishing your body with whole foods and removing inflammatory/processed foods from your diet will naturally increase your intake of antioxidants, which fight free radicals to reduce inflammation in your body. Adapting a healthy, balanced way of eating is essential for long-term physical and mental health.

About the meal plan

The recipes in this meal plan are whole foods based recipes that are simple, quick, and delicious. Each recipe takes under 25 minutes to make and uses no more than 10 ingredients.

Eating a whole food based diet simply means eating foods that have not been messed with or altered chemically or otherwise.
